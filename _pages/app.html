Turn off Play Protect

When you download and install app by third party channel app store or unknown source APK file, the app may be blocked by Google Play Protect and can not be installed, learn how to turn off Play Protect.

Install Android .apk from Unknown Source

  1. Navigate to Setting > Security.
  2. Check the option “Unknown sources“.
  3. Tap OK on the prompt message.
  4. Select “Trust“.
