### Our ‘Aqidah:
We promotes tawhid (monotheism), and the ‘aqidah of Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jama‘ah or the ‘aqidah of the righteous Salaf (early generations of Islam).

### Our Aims: 
1. To spread Islamic Monotheism, Prophetic Sunnah, and presenting the pure Islamic teachings. Allah says: **“That is Allah, your Lord, the Creator of all things, Laa ilaaha illa Huwa (none has the right to be worshipped but He).”** (Quran, Ghafir [40]:62). The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: **“I urge you to adhere to my Sunnah and the way of the Rightly-Guided Caliphs who come after me. Adhere to it and cling firmly to it. And beware of newly introduced matters, for every newly introduced matter is a bid'ah (an innovation), and every bid'ah (in religion) is a going astray.”** [^1], and he ﷺ said; **“Pay attention to my Sunnah (way) and the way of the Rightly-Guided Caliphs after me, adhere to it and hold fast to it.”** [^2], and he ﷺ said in his Farewell Sermon: **“I am leaving behind among you something which, if you adhere to it, you will not go astray after that. It is the Book of Allah (Quran).” The version narrated by al-Hakim says “The Book of Allah (Quran) and my Sunnah.”** [^3] 
2. To enrich the viewers' knowledge and dispel ignorance. Almighty Allah says: **“So ask those who possess knowledge if you do not know”** (Quran, an-Nahl [16]:43).
3. To increase the awareness of Muslims about the mission of Islam.
4. To refute the lies, distortions, and bidahs (innovations in religion), and to warn the people against it. The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said concerning bid'ah: **“Beware of newly introduced matters, for every newly introduced matter is a bid'ah (an innovation), and every bid'ah (in religion) is a going astray.”**

**Finally**, may Allah Ta'ala grant us success and make us steadfast in adhering to the truth and the path of guidance.

[^1]: 
[^2]: Abu Dawud (4607), At-Tirmidhi (2676), Ibn Majah (44)
[^3]: Sahih al-Jami’ by al-Albani 2937
