### Our ‘Aqidah:
We promotes tawhid (pure belief in the Oneness of Allah, monotheism), the ‘aqidah of Ahlus-Sunnah wal-Jama‘ah or the ‘aqidah of the righteous Salaf (early generations of Islam).

### Our Aims: 
1. To presenting the pure Islamic teachings and the Prophetic Sunnah.
2. To enrich the viewers' knowledge and to dispel ignorance. **“So ask those who possess knowledge if you do not know”** (Quran, an-Nahl [16]:43).
3. To increase the awareness of Muslims about the mission of Islam.
4. To refute bidahs (innovations in religion), the lies and distortions. 
5. To warn mankind of God's punishments.

**Finally**, may Allah Ta'ala grant us success and make us steadfast in adhering to the truth and the path of guidance.
