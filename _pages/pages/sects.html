It is not Islam that is divided into sects, rather it is the people who differ and deviated from the Sunnah (way) of their Prophet and deviated from the authentic teachings of Islam. But salvation belongs to only one sect. It was narrated that the Prophet ﷺ said: “My ummah will split into seventy-three sects, one of which will be in Paradise and seventy-two in Hell.” It was said: O Messenger of Allah, who are they? He said: “[The jama’ah](/jamaah-is-in-accordance-with-the-truth) (the main body of the Muslims).” and according to another hadith: “[Those who follow] the path which I and my Companions follow.”
