### Question:
What is the Gharqad (a kind of thorny tree) tree?

### Answer:
This kind of tree is well known in Madinah. The Prophet ﷺ informed us that when the Muslims fight the Jews, the Jews will try to hide behind trees, [every tree will inform the Muslims that a Jew is hiding behind it](https://alsalafiyyah.github.io/hadith-on-stones-will-speak/), except for the Gharqad tree, for it is their tree [^1]. 

[^1]: It is narrated from the hadith of Abu Hurayrah that the Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: **“The Hour will not begin until the Muslims fight the Jews and the Muslims will kill them, until a Jew hides behind a rock or a tree, and the rock or tree will say: 'O Muslim, O slave of Allah, there is a Jew behind me, come and kill him.' Except the gharqad (a thorny tree), for it is one of the trees of the Jews.”** (Sahih Muslim 2922)
