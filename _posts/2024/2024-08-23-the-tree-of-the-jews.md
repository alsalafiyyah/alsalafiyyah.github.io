### Question:
What is the Gharqad tree?

### Answer:
This kind of tree is well known in Madinah. The Prophet ﷺ informed us that when the Muslims fight the Jews, the Jews will try to hide behind trees, every tree will inform the Muslims that a Jew is hiding behind it, except for the Gharqad tree, for it is their tree.
