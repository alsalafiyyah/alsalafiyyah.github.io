### Question: 
Is it Haram (prohibited) or Halal (lawful) to learn the English language?

### Answer: 
If there is a religious or worldly need for learning the English language or other foreign languages, there is no harm in learning it. However, if there is no such need, it is Makruh (disliked) to learn it.
